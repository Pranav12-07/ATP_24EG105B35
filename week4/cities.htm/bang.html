Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is located in the southern part of India and is known for its pleasant climate, cosmopolitan culture, and thriving IT industry. Bangalore is often referred to as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its status as a major hub for technology companies and startups. The city is also home to numerous educational institutions, research centers, and cultural attractions, making it a vibrant and dynamic urban center.
