Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital of the Indian state of Telangana. It is also the de jure capital of Andhra Pradesh, a neighboring state. The city is located on the banks of the Musi River in the Deccan Plateau region of South India. With a population of 10 million, Hyderabad is the fourth most populous city and sixth most populous urban agglomeration in India. It is also the second largest city in South India after Bangalore.
