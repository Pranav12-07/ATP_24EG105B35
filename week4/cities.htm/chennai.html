Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal in South India. Chennai is one of the largest cities in India and serves as a major cultural, economic, and educational hub. The city is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse population. Chennai is also famous for its beautiful beaches, historic landmarks, and thriving film industry, making it a popular destination for tourists and residents alike.
